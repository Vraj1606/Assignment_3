Fact #1

No One Actually Knows Who Invented Them

Frank Galatolie is credited as the creator of the Sour Patch Kids in the 1970s while Galatolie was working as a confectioner at Jaret International in Ontario, California.

Fact #2

Skittles Originated In Ontario

The original candy flavors included lime, lemon, orange and raspberry. Before they were the Sour Patch Kids, the candy was referred to as Mars Men.These deliciously chewy treats were originally named Mars Men to keep up with the space craze during that time. Fittingly, the candies were shaped like martians.
